# KindBite

**KindBite** is a compassionate web application designed to help individuals in need quickly locate nearby free food services, such as food banks, soup kitchens, and community pantries.

Whether you're experiencing financial hardship, homelessness, or food insecurity, KindBite aims to make access to essential nourishment just a few clicks away.

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🔍 **Location-based Search**  
  Search for free food services by postcode or suburb.

- 🗂️ **Service Details**  
  View name, address, opening hours, and special notes for each food support location.

- 🔐 **Simple Login System**  
  Admin users can log in to manage the food service list (add/edit/delete).

- 📱 **Mobile Friendly Design**  
  Fully responsive UI built with Bootstrap 5.

- ☁️ **Powered by Firebase**  
  Uses Firestore for data storage and Firebase Auth for secure login.

---

## 💡 Target Audience

KindBite is created for people experiencing food insecurity — including low-income individuals, immigrants, the homeless, or anyone going through a difficult time. It can also be used by support workers and volunteers to refer clients to the nearest service points.

## 📌 Example Use Case

> Maria is a single mother who recently lost her job. She opens **KindBite**, types in her postcode, and finds two food banks within walking distance that are open today. She heads to one with her ID, gets a meal pack, and is warmly welcomed by volunteers. No registration, no stress — just help when it’s needed.
